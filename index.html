A web app for storing information about your DVD archive.

If you have a stack of DVDs just sitting around, and you'd like a way to inventory them. Then DVD Pila! is for you.



Simple Database

A simple table to contain the meta information about your DVD archive. Easy to integrate the schema into larger applications.

Organize Your DVDs

Know what content you have in your DVD archive, and where it is.

Watch Your Videos

Use Ogg, MP4, or WebM files, and view your collection in any modern browser.
